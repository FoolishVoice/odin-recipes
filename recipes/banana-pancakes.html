banana pancakes

Wake up on the right side of the bed with a stack of sweet, cozy, and simple banana pancakes. The recipe is easy to make and it comes together in just 15 minutes, so you don't have to wake up early to enjoy a satisfying breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • An egg
  • Milk
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Mix your dry ingredients in one bowl and your wet ingredients in another bowl. Add the dry ingredients to the bowl with the wet ingredients, then stir until they're incorporated.
  2. Pour the batter in 1/4 cup portions onto a lightly oiled pan or griddle over medium-high heat. Cook for a few minutes, flip with a spatula, and cook for another few minutes.
  3. Serve your banana pancakes immediately.
