Cookies

This chocolate chip cookie recipe will become your go-to after having a taste. The cookies are soft, chewy, and absolutely irresistible. This is something you can make for almost every occasion.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolalte chips
  • Nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
